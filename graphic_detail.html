Graphic Detail

A detailed vintage clothing app that allows users to add something new to their closet.

The initial project to launch my UI/UX design career was Graphic Detail. Focusing on the base of design, the majority of this project was research-based and was conducted through user testing and viewing other student finalized projects. After research, mood boards and style guides were innovated for a reselling retail app. Graphic Detail is a timeless app that makes cleaning out users' closets simple.
